Let us not forget, running is a chance 

Well practiced, running is health benefits, it promotes the synchronization of body and mind and leads to more spirituality.

"I gave myself, emptied, taken slaps and walls" and I understood that the concept of performance was to be put in the context of his own life and especially combined with the notions of desire and pleasure. You have to put things into perspective ... it's only running ... even if for me, it's rather a great tool to move forward in the life.

Running is an amazing school of life. It teaches us values such as thoroughness, perseverance, surpassing oneself and above all humility. Running does not lie, it is fair and puts you back in your place when you felt too good, too strong

From a balanced and reasoned practice, I seek to improve body and mind knowledge and listening in order to also reap the benefits in everyday life.

Not only do make progress and recover, but a simple jogging can help you refocus on yourself, meditate and subsequently make good choices in your life.

A better self-knowledge helps push limits simply in accepting them with a smile. This principle also applies in personal and professional lives: let's break our own barriers to go further !
