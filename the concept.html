What does "running at the Thai style" mean?
Early in the morning, perfect to start the day and be with your loved ones for breakfast, fresh and available, or at the end of the day, to enjoy the last rays of the sun, without burning out :), and let yourself go.


Running to sensations


Beginner, running fan, runner in full preparation, advanced runner, whatever your level and your motivations, focusing on notions of pleasure and envy, I propose to assist you in your sessions, teaching you the elementary basics for a sustainable practice of running, the ultimate goal of any runner !


In a area marked by hot weather and very high rate of humidity, take the opportunity to run otherwise.
Here, it's hard to keep up the timer , so forget your references and develop your feelings to improve your self-awareness and travel differently!


In Khao Lak and its surroundings


According to your desires and your availability, we will determine the type of session adapted to your level, among various routes within the seaside resort of Khao Lak and its surroundings; 20 km of magnificent beaches overhung by mountainous landscapes in a luxuriant nature, at only 1 hour 15 min from Phuket International Airport.


Learn to run at the feeling with particular emphasis on self-listening, respect for the body and in channeling your mind, for better management of your energy throughout your workouts.


And learn to recover better


It's time to take charge, recovery is a training part very often overlooked by runners.
Indeed, it is essential to allow your body to correctly assimilate the stress of trainings and avoid injuries.
From yoga-based sessions, take the time to find out how to regenerate yourself.



From the single discovery session to the typical training week, through the monitoring of the preparation or the objective, various customized formulas or in mini-group with the same level are proposed to you.



Just lace up your running shoes and be guided, you will leave with a "disoriented" vision of running !
